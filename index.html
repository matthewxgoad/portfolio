About Matt

Midwesterner; world-traveler; stuff-maker.

I started my career journey in Film/Video. I've enjoyed producing content for screens, big and small. I'm a storyteller. Web development is my next, new, exciting frontier to continue engaging people with stories.

The full stack interests me, but front-end user experience is my focus. An intuitive experience is everything to the user.

I'm a natural leader, and I play well with others. I have not problem taking the reigns when needed or relenquishing the spotlight when a teammate is shining.
